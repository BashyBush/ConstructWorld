These are the known bugs/problems that I know about with Construct World.

Mario Related bugs
- Mario can clip through invisible slopes if running.
- Running then letting go of either left or right while holding run will cause Mario to still be in the running animation and freezes on a frame until he fully stops moving.
- Fire Mario is really unfinished and that some animations just does not work sometimes (like holding an item)
- Fireballs doesn't do damage.

Others 
- World Map while somewhat finished. Most parts are unfinished.
- Keyhole ending is unfinished
- Some objects won't pause their animations when pausing.
- Banazi Bills are unfinished.
- Shells are known to randomly rotate if interacting with another shell
